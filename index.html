BT21

BT21 is a South Korean character brand created by line Friends in collaboration with the popular K-pop group BTS. It features a cast of adorable and unique characters, each representing one of the BTS members. The characters have become highly popular and are featured in various merchandise and media, including stickers, clothing, and even a mobile game.

BT21 was created in 2017 as a collaboration between LINE Friends, a subsidiary of Naver Corporation, and BTS, a globally renowned K-pop group. The name "BT21" stands for "BTS Universe Story" and "21st Century," emphasizing the characters' connection to BTS and their modern, trendy appeal.

Overall, BT21 has not only become a lucrative brand but also a cultural phenomenon, blending the popularity of K-pop with cute and relatable characters. It has continued to expand its presence through various collaborations and creative ventures, making it a beloved part of pop culture.
